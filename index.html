About the School

Four intensive days at the frontier of autonomous driving

The ELLIS Summer School on Autonomous Driving is an official ELLIS PhD Program school, approved for 2026. It is designed for PhD students, postdocs, and junior researchers working in the field of autonomous driving and at the intersection of machine learning, computer vision, and robotics.

Hosted at the UAB Casa Convalescència in Barcelona and organized by the Computer Vision Center (CVC), , and ELLIS Unit Barcelona, the school combines expert lectures and panels, demos from labs, and a student poster session.

Research Topics
End-to-End Driving 3D Perception Dataset Curation Sensor Synthesis Foundation Models BEV Perception Reinforcement Learning World Models Sim-to-Real Safety & Robustness SLAM VLA Models
